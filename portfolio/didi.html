Poster Design

The posters is designed for an application called “Juzidui”, which is built to be used within the company. Below are three posters I designed for the first time when the application shown to others,with the application staff in the company could use it to ask questions or get bonus and therefore convenient their life.

This poster come from the idea that the APP would be use inside the company, and it showed some basic scene in such company, giving the image that using it could help handle most life inside the company.As shown in the poster, first Floor is Parking Lot, and second is canteen, third is office, and forth is gym.

The poster shows that it could be use under some emergency situation(say, you're run out of toilet paper inside toilet or something else goes wrong....)
