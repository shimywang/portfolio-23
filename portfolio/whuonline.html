How unconvenient life could be in a school over 3,5000 m² ?

Life could be hard sometimes while living in such a large campus. With a history of over 120 years and being one of the most historic universities in China, there are too much departments that a student could probably unable to memorize or get to know.

There are also problems like your lessons could let you go through the whole campus, for example, when taking an elective class, I may need to go from one building to another for more than 40 mintues, and not knowing where’s the next class will make you hard to get there in time(which always happens).

WHU Online is an iOS app built to help students in Wuhan University(WHU) to live a more convenient life, like getting imformations they need and offer some useful (or better) services. The last version of WHU Online was designed in 2013, and some part of it had been considered out of time and needed to be refreshed.

Evaluation

After making the prototype, we conducted an interview on 9 students around us, beccause they are people who finally get to use the app, and things are still unsure for us.

6/9 told us that informations in the”News” tab is not so useful as those in the “Course” tab, and that made us decided to change the sequence from “News - Course - Life - My” to “Course - News -Life -My”

5/9 told us it is not unconvenient to see there schedule of the week after a lot of clicking (actually 2 times) . so wechanged it into a page with a tab on top of it in which users can quickly view both kinds of there schedules. And we also decided to move the entrance of checking scores from “Courses” tab to “Life” tab.

Further improvement

We rearranged the sequence of our four tabs, and changed some entrances of our services.Because the services for libraries had been possible, we had added some new parts of functions in “Life”. Since most people also said that our logo for WHU Online was hard to understand and not representative enough, we redesigned the whole logo.
