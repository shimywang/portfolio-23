Service Design: Service strategy innovation and analysis of digital touchpoints for a mainstream automobile brand

Service Design, Customer Journey, Mobile APP, UI, Walkthrough

2022

Shanghai,China

This project aimed to redesign mobile application and related digital touchpoints of a famous Chinese car brand, in order to help imcrease their potential customers from online channels.
As a result, our design and strategies has been launched by our customers.

1 What are we designing for ?

After years of development, our client has build a complicated system to presents their information and provide services for purchasing car. However, their service and information systems didn’t help them much on improving online sells. They still rely heavily on their offline stores and sales.

Another problem is that with their existing online services and information systems, they lack the consistency between different systems and channels, and their own brand identity when comparing to their competitors.

The brand hoped to redesign their own online selling service and process, so that more potential customers could be attracted and therefore improve their sales on online channel.

Key Challenges

1.There are multiple stakeholders involved in this project, we need to create a new strategy/solution that all these stakeholders would be happy with.

2. We need to create a differentiated and memorable online service experience, while most competitors on the market are highly homogenized.

2 Process

3 What we've done

01 Desktop Research

After analyzing the project goal, we focus on how to increase sales via digital services to develop our desktop research.

We specifically focus on trends and cases of how to increase the potential users attracted to the brand, and how to reduce existing user loss.

02 Organization & stakeholders analysis

We analyzed how service providers & participants interacts, and visualized the relationship between multiple service providers into ecosystem map and stakeholders map.

We also analyzed user research data, developed customer-journey map and service blueprints. All these provide supports our further study on touchpoint and service strategy.

03 UX Walkthrough

We designed tasks and conducted cognitive walkthrough based on user research data & desktop research data.

Combined with heuristic walkthrough result, we provide findings on how to further improve current services.

04 Service Strategy

Based on findings in UX walkthrough, we came up with multiple strategy alternatives.

With feedback from clients and co-design workshop, we finalized our service strategies.

05 Strategy Refine

We add more detailed descriptions to different strategy directions. This includes specifying touchpoint and user scenario, provides storyboards and descriptions of key values of each service and experience.

06 Service launching manual

We developed service launching manual for multiple different stakeholders, based on our service strategies.

This manual includes:
i. What we want to achieve with each service strategy
ii. For each service strategy, introducing service principles, service instructions and the overall workflow.
iii. For each service strategy, provides examples of how it could be launched, including UX and UI.
