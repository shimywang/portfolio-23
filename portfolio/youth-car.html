Product Strategy: Designing a car for young adults

Product strategy, User Research, Innovation, Automobile

2020

Shanghai,China

This project works with a famous autonomous brand in China. Our client hope to find out a new product strategy & innovative concept, to attract young adults who just graduated and want to purchase their very first car.

1 Process

2 Outcome

01 Desktop Research

Insights on young adults’ lifestyle.
Insights on unique values for young adults and their vehicles.

02 Persona

Create 4 Personas for the product, which are representative and impactful for the designer teams to design a car for young adults, based on extreme users we found in interviews.

Personas are used as material for generating key insights and ideas.

03 Key insights

Generate key insights on how to possibly create unique value for young products according to our user research data & desktop research data.

04 Product feature strategy

After workshop with clients for generating ideas, we evaluate, select, refine and regroup ideas to generate final solution of product feature strategy.

05 Service Strategy

Explore further on user scenario and customer journey based on product feature strategy. Add possible service experience & extra accessories feature design for creating better product experience.
