A graduation gift designed for my friends

In 2016, some of my friends graduated from Wuhan University and I decieded to design some special gifts for them to remember the precious 2 or 3 years we spend together.
As they were leaving the university and cleaning up their dormitory, I don’t want my gift bother them much, so it won’t be too large or too heavy to keep, also, as we won’t meet again in the recent years, I also hope the gift I gave them would be able to remain for a period of time, which means that they couldn’t be subject that is too fragile and can be easily damaged......
A key-chain is a perfect choice for my gift, and give me enough space to create unique key-chain for my friends, to share my memories with my friends.
Here is the draft of the key-chain I designed for my friends.

This is the tradition look of WHU’s students in the 1930s, the umbrella in sakura shape also represents our university. I designed this for my friend Wenzhu Chen because I took photograph with her in this look in 2015.(Love those photos)

Shifeng loves motorbike and told me it was his dream to ride motorbike in the night, and that’s what we did together. Hope it was a great memory to him:)

Guanzhang love cats and there is a cat living in our dormitary, Everytime I see her in the dormitary she was playing with the cat. The dress the girl wearing is a piece of dress we bought together in Hangzhou.
