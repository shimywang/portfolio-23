Innovation on wall oven: Evlove better for attracting online-shopping customers

Product Strategy, User Rearch, Home Appliance

2021

Shanghai,China

Result:

We helped client increase growth of 14.3% in sales in 2022 with product innovation strategy.

Our client is one of the top high-end home appliance brands in China. By this project, we wish to create next generation wall ovens, which could help our clients to increase the single product sales, instead of the current status of rely heavily on selling as a set with range hoods.

With the clear trend that people in China tend to purchase almost everything online while there hadn’t been any oven products specifically for online customers, our client want to focus specifically on online channels and create a new product that can help them stand out.

1 Thinking on the problem

Our main focus on this project is to innovate on the wall ovens for online channels. To answer this question, we have two key problems to focus on:

01 How do online customers purchase wall ovens?

With the blooming of e-commerce in China, there are more customers rely heavily on online shopping and get used to finishing all purchase steps, from knowing the product to purchasing the product online. Compare to the traditional shopping decision-making process, below are the two main different decision-making model of today’s new customers compare to the traditional model:

I. Even shorter decision-making time: They buy when they know it

II. Even longer decision-making time: Compare everything on social media

02 What are the differences between single product consumer & range hood sets consumer?

It’s much more convenient to purchase home appliances as a set than selecting every single product from multiple different brands.

Therefore, as we aim at increasing sales of single product, we need to create really attractive product, that can differentiate from other products or brands so that customers are willing to buy the single product with extra efforts.

2 Prolem to solve

In order to improve sales on online channel, we need to create product that can be easily recognized and differentiated from competitors through online channel.

This new product should be able to quickly attract consumers by simply viewing its introductions online, ideally without any helps from offline channel, which can be solved by creating features from the two following aspects.

Product Feature

Should be easily perceived by consumers and hits their painpoints.

Product Exterio

Should be significantly different from its competitors and able to stand out with even one glimpse.

4 Outcome

Focus on the full experience while cooking

Although cooking appliance serve mainly for cooking delicious food, but users are not only focusing on how delicious food they could make. Their needs and experience towards these cooking appliance also includes:

Experience while using it - Are they easy to use, which includes:
1.Interacting with its controller parts for adjusting time &temperature: e.g. How to adjust? Is it easy to adjust?

2.Interaction with the machine while cooking: e.g. How to open the oven? How’s the experience of putting food into the oven?

Its appearance - How does it look? Does it fit in with my home?

Concepts launched

1. Between touch screen and knob

Users mostly start to adjust settings or select cooking modes after they’ve finished pre-processing their food. However, as their hands are often wet and dirty at this time, it is much more complicated for them to use touch screen because they need to consequently wipe or clean up their hands.

In addition, most users want to be able to finish adjusting settings as soon as possible, while these wall-ovens often provide a wide range and delicate way to adjust, such as adjusting temperature from 30°C to 230°C by 1°C, or adjusting cooking time from 1 min to 180 mins by 1min, making users hard to achieve their goals.

Users need a quicker and simpler way to control their ovens. Although touch screen was the absolute mainstream at that time, it requires users to click several dozens of times for setting up. However, for the traditional knobs, they still stands for the out-dated products to user. Users still wish to have the element of digital screens to showcase their high-end product is smart and used advanced technology, while they also want a quicker and easier way to control their machine.

Therefore, we have come up with the idea to combine knob with digital screens. This new Smart knob allows user to use rotary knob to quickly select modes and adjust settings, which is much quicker than pointing at screens, its screen on the knob tells clearly what users select, which helps user to control in a easier and more precise way, while providing a ‘smarter’ feeling of interaction to users.

2. Cooling down steam

When coming to user experience, it is also about how users interact with the machine while cooking, which might not be directly connected to how good your food taste.

Most products would keep their focus on how better foods could be with larger volume steams. However, larger volume steam also comes with a worse user experience. When opening the oven for taking food, there is always hot steam spilling out of the oven and running directly into users’ face. What’s worse, there are steam, which could have very high temperature, escaping from oven during the long cooking process even though the door is perfectly closed, making the oven the worst place to stand close by or walk around. In summary, users have really bad experiences in the following 3 circumstances: when taking/putting food, when observing the cooking status, and when moving around in their own kitchen while the machine is working.

It is valuable to work further on how to relieve the pain of being affected by the spilled steam. For experienced users, they can easily imagine the scenario of how annoying the spilled steam is, and get attracted to the product on how this feature could make their cooking life easier. With such feature and strategy of continually emphasizing and working further on the steam problem, we think there might be a higher possibility for our customers to better differentiate from competitors and stand out.

3. Adding value to ovens: externalize its cooking ability

Wall oven takes a considerably large space in kitchen. For most people, they would like to utilize every tiny space in their home. More things they could do in the same space, the better it is. Therefore, it would strongly attracts potential users if an oven product could do more, being multifunction and be even more professional.

On the other hand, although combi oven can be used for cooking skills way more than simply just steaming buns or bakery, most entry level users wouldn’t be able to think of it. Without actual experience of using ovens for stews or gilled meals, beginners would often consider they might only have very few occasions to use such product and therefore not worthy.

To convert such thoughts of combi oven can hardly be used, we think it is important to externalize its ability, so that users can understand combi ovens can also be multifunction at their first glimpse. Creating specialized features or accessories for specified cooking techniques could also help users to imagine that they could get even more professional with the help of such product.

Therefore, we created oven accessories, such as stew pots, grill net, rotating grill to further externalize its cooking techniques, making the multifunction feature of combi oven much more obvious to attract potential customers.
