About

Hi there! I am Yuyan :D
I’m a experienced design strategist and had served for multiple famous brands in home appliances & automobiles in China. I have received my education in a combination of design, engineering and business, with solid skills leading and conducting design projects in multiple fields.

With my strong curiosity and quick learning skills, I am confidence in entering new fields and seeking for best design solutions.

For me, the most important part of design is interaction: that how to deliver a better way for users to interact with the product, or to interact with the service. By focusing on creating simpler yet better interactions, we can create more powerful product solutions, or stronger service solutions.
